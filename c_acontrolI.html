This course, Modern Control Engineering (Control 1), bridges the gap between classical methods and modern state-space representation, focusing on the stability, estimation, and control of complex MIMO systems.

📐 State-Space Theory & Stability:

Transitioning from Transfer Functions to Multi-Variable State-Space Analysis.

  • System Foundations: Automatic control review and introduction to Digital Control systems.
  • Stability Analysis: Rigorous study of Lyapunov Stability Theory for linear and nonlinear systems.
  • Structural Properties: Evaluating Controllability and Observability matrices to ensure system reachability and trackability.

🚀 Controller Synthesis & Estimation:

State Feedback
  • Pole Placement Techniques
  • Full-State Feedback Control
  • Ackermann’s Formula
  • Regulator Design
Observer Design
  • Full-Order Observers
  • State Estimation Logic
  • Observer-Based Control
  • Separation Principle

💻 Integrated MATLAB Projects:

Implementation of State-Feedback Controllers with State Observers for:
• Automotive: Lateral Vehicle Dynamics & Active Suspension Systems.
• Robotics: SCARA Robot & 2-DOF Robotic Arm trajectory control.
• Process Control: Flow Control in Interconnected Tank systems.
