This advanced curriculum provides a rigorous mathematical framework for the Stability Analysis and Control of Time-Delay Systems, focusing on Lyapunov-based methods and Linear Matrix Inequalities (LMIs) for robust industrial applications.

📊 Modeling & MATLAB Simulation:

Developing precise models for state, input, and neutral delays.

  • System Classes: Modeling Constant, Time-Varying, Distributed, and Neutral delay systems.
  • Domain Analysis: Frequency response, transfer functions, and controllability/observability of delayed plants.
  • Numerical Tools: Step-by-step simulation of complex delay dynamics in MATLAB.

🛡️ Stability Theory & LMI Toolbox:

Lyapunov Methods
  • Lyapunov-Krasovskii Functionals
  • Razumikhin Stability Theorem
  • Wirtinger-based Inequalities
  • Reciprocally Convex Approaches
LMI Conditions
  • Delay-Dependent/Independent LMIs
  • Free-Weighting Matrix Method
  • Polytopic Uncertainties
  • Stability of Descriptor Systems

🚀 Robust Control & Modern Applications:

  • Performance Metrics: $H_\infty$ Filtering, $L_2$-gain analysis, and Passivity for delayed dynamics.
