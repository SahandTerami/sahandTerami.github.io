About Me

Researcher, Programmer, Tutor, and Dream Chaser

Holding an M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from K. N. Toosi University, I have experience collaborating with international research teams in the United States.

My research primarily focuses on Control Theory, Optimal Control (particularly Model Predictive Control), Learning-Based Control, Optimization, Data-Driven Modeling, Autonomous Vehicles, Multi-Agent Systems, and Complex Systems. Currently, my primary focus is on developing robust and optimal data-driven control and modeling architectures using reservoir computing and reinforcement learning, with applications in connected cars and autonomous vehicles. My work combines simulation and implementation, which I carry out using MATLAB, Julia, and C++.
