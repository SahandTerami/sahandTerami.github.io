A comprehensive masterclass in Genetic Algorithms (GA) and Evolutionary Computing, bridging the gap between biological inspiration and industrial-grade optimization using MATLAB.

🧬 Evolutionary Engine & GA Mechanics:

Deep exploration of GA operators for Binary, Discrete, and Continuous search spaces.

  • Selection Strategies: Roulette Wheel, Tournament Selection (Pressure Analysis), and Boltzmann Distribution.
  • Advanced Crossover: Single/Double point, Uniform, and Arithmetic Crossover with Out-of-Bound parameters.
  • Mutation Dynamics: Gaussian (Normal) mutation and the One-Fifth Rule for step-size adaptation.
  • Performance Metrics: Tracking NFE (Number of Function Evaluations) and convergence behaviors.
