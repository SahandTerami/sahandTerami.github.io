This comprehensive module covers the Theory and Application of Genetic Algorithms (GA), moving from biological foundations to solving high-impact engineering and industrial optimization problems using MATLAB.

🧬 Genetic Mechanics & Operators:

A deep dive into the evolutionary loop for Binary, Discrete, and Continuous search spaces.

  • Selection Logic: Roulette Wheel, Tournament Selection, and Boltzmann-based probability distributions.
  • Advanced Crossover: Single/Double point, Uniform, and Arithmetic Crossover with expansion parameters.
  • Mutation & Adaptation: Gaussian mutation and step-size control via the One-Fifth Rule.
  • Convergence Analysis: Tracking NFE (Number of Function Evaluations) and termination criteria.

🏗️ Optimization Case Studies:

Logistics & Operations
  • Hub Location Allocation (HLA)
  • Transportation & Supply Chain
  • Quadratic Assignment (QAP)
  • Knapsack Combinatorics
Dynamic Modeling
  • Nonlinear System Identification
  • Predator-Prey (Lotka-Volterra)
  • ODE Simulation (Euler Method)
  • Permutation-based Search

Technical Focus: Constraint Handling

Implementation of advanced Penalty Functions (Additive, Multiplicative, and Mixed) to map constrained real-world problems into feasible evolutionary search spaces, with specialized support for Permutation-based coding (Swap, Reversion, Insertion).
