This specialized course focuses on Vehicle Dynamics Analysis using CarSim and CarSim, providing a high-fidelity environment to simulate vehicle performance, safety protocols, and control algorithm development.

🚛 Vehicle Modeling & Subsystem Configuration:

Defining high-degree-of-freedom mathematical models for light and heavy vehicles.

  • Structural Parameters: Configuring mass distribution, aerodynamics, and precise center of gravity (CoG) calculations (e.g., Iveco models).
  • Mechanical Subsystems: Detailed modeling of Powertrains, Axle configurations, and Suspension kinematics.
  • Tire Dynamics: Implementing complex tire-road interaction models for various friction coefficients.
  • Load Management: Defining cargo specifications and their impact on vehicle stability.

🛣️ Driving Scenarios & Safety Testing:

Dynamic Maneuvers
  • Off-road Performance Testing
  • Tilt Table Stability Analysis
  • Braking & Steering Response
  • Crosswind Impact Simulation
Safety Systems
  • ABS & Traction Control
  • Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
  • Rollover Prevention Analysis
  • Driver-in-the-loop Modeling

💻 MATLAB/Simulink Co-Simulation:

Mastering the CarSim-Simulink Interface to develop and validate external control algorithms. This integration allows for real-time data exchange between high-fidelity vehicle models and custom controller architectures for advanced ADAS research.
